What is GreenOps?

GreenOps is about designing, building and improving solutions in the cloud. The main goal is to define, measure and reduce Carbon emissions, and reduce energy waste.

The main problem statements are:

1) Major Cloud Providers advertise that their data centres are Carbon Neutral - however; is this not an accurate picture.

2) Cloud Provider Telemetry is enabling Greenwashing - reporting that customers have a "zero" cloud footprint.

3) Cloud Carbon emissions have exceeded the automotive and shipping industry.
